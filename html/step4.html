Sorry! You didn't select a ring metal.



Gold is a natural, yellow metal. 100% pure gold is too soft for ring settings. Thus gold rings are mixed with other metals (e.g., silver, copper) to give it strength and durability.

White Gold is like Gold, but coated with a white metal (e.g., rhodium, palladium). This outer layer will eventually have to be re-coated every few years when it wears out.

Platinum is a white metal, more rare and denser than Gold. Because of its rarity and density, Platinum is 2 - 3 times more expensive than Gold or White Gold.
