Our Story

Sparklie traces its origin to the fall of 2012, when a friend took his first step towards marriage and started searching online for that perfect diamond engagement ring. What he found was an avalanche of information, but nothing directly helped him find the ideal ring given his budget. Hearing his fustrations start Sparklie, an online tool to help you find that perfect ring. One year later and lots of pizza, coffee, and late night drinks, the idea behind Sparklie is finally coming together.
