About Sparklie

Sparklie traces its origin to the fall of 2012, when a close friend took his first step towards marriage and started searching for his perfect engagement ring. What he found was an avalanche of information and lots of frustration. We started Sparklie to help him and every man discover the perfect engagement ring.

DISCOVER THE STYLE


We understand that finding a ring can be hard. You're overwhelmed with information, options, and decisions. So, we've created a style guide that helps uncover the right ring from cool brands. It's simple and free.



HELP ALONG THE WAY


After reviewing different rings, you might need some help narrowing down your choices. That's why our jewelry experts can help. Each of our experts is trained by the Gemological Institute of America, the world's foremost authority on gems. They'll answer all your questions and guide you along the way. They aren't compensated by commission, so they won't try to upsell you.



VIEW IN STORES


Sure, we can ship it to your doorstep. But we know you want to see the ring first. That's why we've partnered with select, local retailers. We'll help schedule your appointment and handle all your needs so you'll have a great experience.
